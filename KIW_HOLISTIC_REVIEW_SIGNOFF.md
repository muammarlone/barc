## 2. Pillar Verification

### Pillar 1: Governed Autonomy (DYNAMIC_RACI_GOVERNANCE_SPEC)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The integration of the Dynamic RACI Engine (DRE) with the Variety Matrix ensures that every system action is mapped to a human or agentic owner. The "Substitution Logic" and "Staff-to-Staff Collaboration" layer provide the necessary resilience for complex enterprise environments.

### Pillar 2: Sensory Integration (ENTERPRISE_INTEGRATION_PLUGINS)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The multi-channel ingestion strategy (DMS, Email, Collab Hub) and the categorical logic of the Email Ingestor successfully bridge the gap between BPO evidence and internal assessment DSAs.

### Pillar 3: Technical Rigor (KIW_ENHANCED_SPEC / SPECIALTY_LAB_SPEC)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The use of Domain Specialist Agents (DSAs) calibrated against "Golden Standards" in a private Specialty Lab ensures that technical findings are not only accurate but also consistent with enterprise best practices.

### Pillar 4: Communication Integrity (COMMUNICATION_PROTOCOL_SPEC)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The PMO Distribution Gate and the Communication Specialist Agent (CSA) provide a robust firewall between internal technical debate and external executive reporting. Layered (L1-L3) reporting ensures McKinsey-style clarity for leadership.

### Pillar 5: Operational Continuity (BPO_LIFECYCLE_OPERATIONS_SPEC)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The inclusion of Project Charter extraction, Change Request (CR) management, and Peak Season Readiness planning ensures that BARC manages the *entire* BPO lifecycle, not just point-in-time audits.

### Pillar 6: Ethical & Security Integrity (ZERO_TRUST_ETHICAL_AI_SPEC)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The implementation of Zero Trust principles for agent identities and the mandatory "Reasoning Trace" for AI findings guarantee that the system is secure, unbiased, and 100% explainable.

### Pillar 7: Productivity & Automation (FS_GENERATOR_SPEC)
- **Status**: **PASSED**
- **Findings**: The FS Asset Factory dramatically reduces documentation overhead by formalizing multi-modal requirements into structured specifications with built-in traceability.
