# FOUNDATION (Authoritative Memory) - BARC Project

**Status**: Authoritative / Immutable  
**Purpose**: Vision, mission, values, and non-negotiables for the BPO Architecture Review Copiolet (BARC).

## Mission
To transform BPO onboarding from a manual, error-prone spreadsheet exercise into a transparent, agent-assisted, and fully auditable digital workflow that guarantees 99.99% compliance before go-live.

## Problem statement
BPO onboarding currently suffers from:
- **Lack of Governance**: Manual reviews are inconsistent and slow.
- **Fragmented Evidence**: Logs, diagrams, and configs are scattered across emails and DMS.
- **Communication Gaps**: Technical findings are not effectively translated for executive leadership.
- **Audit Blindspots**: No immutable record of why an architecture was approved.

## Solution statement
BARC is a **KIW-Grade Governed Agentic System** that:
- Deploys **Domain Specialist Agents (DSAs)** to automate technical deep-dives.
- Uses a **Communication Specialist Agent (CSA)** to bridge technical and business gap.
- Enforces **Separation of Powers** via the GADOS protocol.
- Captures a **Golden Record** of the entire BPO lifecycle.

## Core values (non-negotiable)
- **Truth over speed**: No BPO is "Onboarded" until verified with hard evidence.
- **Artifacts over memory**: All governance decisions must be recorded in the `gados-project` vault.
- **Zero Trust**: Continuous verification of all project identities (Human & Agent).
- **Ethical AI**: 100% explainability for every architect finding.
- **Audit-ready always**: Decision and evidence are traceable and defensible.

## Definition of “Verified”
A feature or assessment finding is `VERIFIED` only when:
- It is backed by evidence stored in the `Evidence Layer`.
- It has been peer-reviewed by a `Thinking Agent` or a Senior Architect.
- It has passed through the `PMO Distribution Gate`.
- The verifier (Delivery Governor) is independent of the implementer.

## Authorities
- **Human Authority (Senior Architect)**: Final authority over assessment overrides, ethics, and "Go/No-Go" decisions.
- **Delivery Governor (VDA)**: Final authority over verification and certification of truth within the GADOS workflow.
- **Communication Owner (PMO)**: Final authority over external distribution of artifacts.
